Step By Step

Interactive dance station

by Kult

"Step by Step" is an engaging interactive station designed to instruct gallery visitors in the intricate footwork of Malay traditional dances, specifically Ronggeng and Zapin. The station employs precisely timed light projections of footprints to guide participants through the fundamental steps of these dances. This installation utilizes relay modules to synchronize the projections with a tutorial video, ensuring an immersive and educational experience.
