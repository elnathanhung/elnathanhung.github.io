“Monkey See, Monkey Do” refers to the act of imitation with the limited knowledge of consequences or reason. Playing on the idea that a small ripple can create a tsunami, Whackinator serves as a commentary on the societal need for inclusion and the hive mind mentality. The hands are sets of servo motors programmed with Raspberry Pi computers while the hand sensing is through Python Mediapipe which communicates to the servos via OSC message.
