Analog Dreams

Projection Mapping Experience

Analog Dreams by Off The Beaten Track x Kult is a dynamic projection mapping experience where abstract animated sequences are mapped onto a sculptural arrangement of stacked and offset cuboids. Using the trompe-l’œil technique, it creates an illusion of depth and shifting dimensions when viewed from a precise central vantage point.

Narrative Journey:

  1. Hello World – The future is uncertain, yet hopeful.
  2. The Digital Age – A vibrant, colorful burst capturing the energy of technology.
  3. Where We Are Meant to Be – A harmonious resolution where everything falls into place.

Created in TouchDesigner and operated through live, manual interactions, each performance becomes a unique, responsive expression of the narrative.
