“Retro Future” is a World Building Game run on Unreal Engine 4. Imagine reliving your day over and over again. In a post apocalyptic future, robots dominate over society. Humans are left to die while cyborgs and robots triumph. Due to the war, the world becomes self-destructive and civilisation is soon brought to extinction.

Our protagonist needs to find a way to prevent the invention of AI from ever happening. With time-travel technology, he need to find the creator and convince him/her to stop the invention.
