A choose-your-own-adventure interactive narrative game

The Day Before unfolds as an engaging 'choose-your-own-adventure' narrative and interactive experience, drawing inspiration from the thought-provoking film "Primer" (2004). Crafted using TouchDesigner, the game employs containers and buttons to create an intuitive user interface with diverse paths. Step into the shoes of an inventive protagonist grappling with financial constraints, desperately seeking to secure a lottery win. To achieve this, you must navigate a unique process of losing first, discovering the winning number, time-traveling back to the day before, and submitting the victorious digits. However, the success of your endeavor comes with unforeseen and dire consequences such as glitches in the fabric of time.
